Membership Levels

NP (Non-Profit) Membership: No Fee

  • Chamber Business Directory (one mailing list request/year)
  • Invitation to participate in Chamber committees or task forces
  • Member spotlight (featured on social media platforms)

Bronze Membership: $700

  • Everything in NP Membership
  • Anniversary/Grand Opening/Ribbon Cutting (one request)
  • Four registrations to each Business After Hours event
  • Annual consultation with a Business Development team member

Silver Membership: $1,100

  • Everything in NP and Bronze memberships
  • Anniversary/Grand Opening/Ribbon Cutting (two requests)
  • Two registrations to Annual Legislative Reception
  • Company advertisement on Chamber website

Gold Membership: $1,500

  • Everything in NP, Bronze, and Silver memberships
  • Priority invitation to register for Annual Washington, D.C. Trip
  • Two registrations to each Policy Forum
  • Access to Town Hall Conference Call during legislative session

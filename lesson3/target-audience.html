Target Audience

The target audience for this site is people who own businesses that operate in an near Concrete, Washington. Those who are already members of the Concrete Chamber of Commerce will use the site, but business owners who haven't yet joined the Chamber of Commerce will also use the site. Community members who want to learn more about the Concrete Chamber of Commerce will likely also use the site. Another group is visitors to Concrete that might want to learn more about the community, although visitors are not as likely to use the site as business owners.

Purpose

This site is meant to showcase the Chamber of Commerce for the small town of Concrete, Washington. The site will also allow users to find businesses in the area. The site will include historical information and information about city events. Businesses can sign up for the Chamber here, and the "Join" page will include different membership levels for businesses. There will also be contact information for the Chamber.
